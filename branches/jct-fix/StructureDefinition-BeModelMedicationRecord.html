Logical Model: Medication Record Logical Model

Official URL: http://hl7belgium.org/fhir/riziv-medicationrecord/StructureDefinition/BeModelMedicationRecord Version: 0.1.0-test
Active as of 2022-11-14 Responsible: HL7 Belgium Realm: Computable Name: BeModelMedicationRecord

A model for a structure collection of a patient’s medication history.
